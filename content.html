ABOUT US

NISB is the IEEE Student Branch of The National Institute of Engineering.

NISB conducts various kinds of workshops, seminars, webinars, technical talks, competitions, hackathons, and events througout the semester to help its members for the all round development with the advancing technology.

Having been active for a decade, we have been honoured and humbled with numerous awards and accolades over time, including "The Best Student Chapter" of Region 10 Bangalore. We organize and host a wide range of technical workshops and events.
